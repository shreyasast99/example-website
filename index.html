Climate Change

We are trying to promote awareness of global warming as a worldwide problem. This is an extremely important problem that needs to be addressed in a drastic way. Global warming causes ice caps to melt resulting in smaller shorelines and severe flooding. This sharp increase in temperature can be cause of harsh droughts that persist for long periods of time. For example, California is going through the worst drought in 1200 years because of global warming. Acute warming disrupts coral ecosystem and precipitates animal extinction. In addition to these terrible consequences, increases in temperature can precipitate a breeding ground for pathogens and vectors that carry them. Global warming is a rampant problem in our world today, and it is our duty to push awareness for it.
